# Privacy Policy — Model Health Auditor

_Publisher: BIM Guard • Last updated: 2026 • Contact: mahmoud.ossama89@gmail.com

This policy explains what data the Model Health Auditor application ("the App") collects, how it is
used, who it is shared with, how long it is kept, and how you can revoke consent or request
deletion. It is written to satisfy the Autodesk App Store ("Design and Make Marketplace")
publisher requirements.

## 1. What data the App collects, how, and how it is used

**Processed locally, never transmitted.** Your Revit models, IFC/IDS files, element data,
parameter values, and the validation reports you generate are read and processed **entirely on
your own computer**. The App does **not** send any model or project data to Gallium Arch or to any
third party. Reports you export are written only to the file location you choose.

**Collected for licensing (the only data transmitted).** When you activate or validate a paid
license, the App transmits the following to the licensing service, solely to verify your license:
- your **license key** (which you entered), and
- a **non-personal machine fingerprint** — a one-way hash derived from machine/user identifiers,
  used only to node-lock the license to your device.

No model content, file names, personal name, or email is collected by the App during normal use.
The 30-day trial is tracked locally on your machine and requires no network connection.

## 2. Third parties and equivalent protection

The App shares the limited licensing data above only with the licensing/payment provider that
processes your purchase and activation (for example the Autodesk App Store entitlement service, or
a licensing provider such as LicenseSeat or Cryptolens, and the Merchant of Record for direct
sales). Where such third parties handle this data, they are contractually required to provide a
level of data protection at least equivalent to that described in this policy and to use the data
only to validate and support your license. The App contains **no advertising networks, no
analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking**.

## 3. Data retention and deletion

- The **machine fingerprint** is a one-way hash and is not used to identify individuals; it is
  retained by the licensing service only while your license is active.
- **License key and activation records** are retained for the duration of your license plus the
  period required for support, accounting, and legal/tax compliance, after which they are deleted
  or anonymised.
- **Local trial/license tokens** stored on your machine under
  `%ProgramData%\Gallium\ModelHealthAuditor` are removed when you deactivate or uninstall.

## 4. Revoking consent and requesting deletion

- You can **revoke consent at any time** by deactivating the license in the App
  (**About / License → deactivate**) and uninstalling the App, which removes all local tokens.
- You can request **access to or deletion of** the licensing records associated with your key by
  emailing **mahmoud.ossama89@gmail.com**; verified requests are actioned within 30 days, subject to
  legal retention obligations.

## 5. Children
The App is a professional engineering tool and is not directed at children.
